WHO WE ARE

RIBROD CAPITAL is a Private Equity firm specialised in the healthcare sector.

We seek to invest in the pharmaceutical and biotech verticals.

Our main focus is to invest in small and medium - sized mature business.

We provide equity capital in flexible and creative capital structures.

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

Our target companies generally meet the following criteria

Revenues of $5M to $50M

at least $500K in free cashflow

In business al least five years, with a minimum of three years of prior financial statements

Principal operations in Spain, we will selectively consider transactions outside of Spain

Strong management team, either incumbent or previously identified as part of an acquisition

WHAT WE OFFER

Our team has the investment experience to structure deals to meet the varied needs of management teams and business owners

  • Flexible acquisition, partial or full buyout
  • Debt consolidation
  • Recapitalisation
  • Corporate divestitures

Marc Riba Founder & CEO

Marc Riba started RIBROD CAPITAL in order to give continuation and expansion to small healthcare companies that had no plans for scalability. Marc has vast knowledge of scaling and growing healthcare businesses as he is also the director of Bluementech, a neuropharmacological development company that has just closed a multi-million dollar deal for the license of their patent portfolio.

Gerard de Lucas Legal Counsel
